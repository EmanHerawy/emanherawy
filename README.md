<h2 align="left" font-weight="bold">About me</h2>  
<br><br>

---

**Eman Herawy** is a blockchain developer and smart contract security auditor with 6+ years of hands-on experience across various blockchain platforms. Eman began her journey in the blockchain space in 2017, specializing in smart contract development using Solidity, and has expanded into security auditing over the past year to help strengthen blockchain protocols and protect user funds.

Her expertise spans multiple programming languages including **Solidity (advanced)**, **Rust**, and **Move (beginner)**, enabling her to build robust, multi-chain solutions. Eman has won **10+ international Web3 hackathons**, demonstrating exceptional problem-solving skills and deep understanding of blockchain technology.

As a smart contract auditor, Eman completed an internship at **Nethermind** where she audited 10+ projects, identifying vulnerabilities classified as Critical, High, Medium, Low, and Informational. She continues to sharpen her skills through public audit contests on **CodeHawks**, **Sherlock**, and **Cantina**, where she has identified **9 high-severity** and **1 low-severity** vulnerabilities.

Eman is a **Chainlink Developer Expert** with hands-on experience integrating CCIP, VRF, Price Feeds, Functions, and Automation. She has also earned the **Macro Smart Contract Security Fellowship Badge** and **RACED Badge**, validating her commitment to security excellence.

Beyond technical work, Eman is passionate about community empowerment. She founded **Arabs in Blockchain**, the largest Arabic-speaking Web3 community, and co-founded **NoonDAO**, the first Arab women-led DAO empowering Arab women in blockchain technology.

Eman is a **TechWomen Emerging Tech Leader (2022)** and **Devcon Scholar Alumni**, holding a Master's degree in Cloud Computing Networks from Cairo University (2020).
